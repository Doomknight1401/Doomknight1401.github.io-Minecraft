Instructions for Minecraft voice chat

Setup for voice chat in Minecraft

  1. Download the fabric-installer and the 2 mods below
  2. Double click the fabric-installer and make sure "client" is selected at the top as well as the latest minecraft version and loader version. Keep the "Launcher location" as it is. Make sure "Create profile" is ticked. Then simply hit install (when it's done you can close and even delete the fabric installer)
  3. Open Minecraft and go to installations
  4. Click on the folder icon on the right side of the fabric loader installation. (Hover over it to reveal the icons)
  5. In the window that just popped up, go to the mods folder (if there is none, make one called "mods"), put these 2 mods in there. You don't have to click on them, just put them in that folder
  6. You can now play Minecraft (make sure the forge loader installation is selected when you hit play)

Using voice chat in game

  1. Press V to open the voice chat menu
  2. Click on "Select Microphone" and select your microphone. Do the same for speaker.
  3. You can enable Minecrophone testing to check if you can hear yourself and if everything works properly. (Activate noise supression if there's background noise)
  4. You can change the activation type: Push to talk or Voice
  5. You can change the controls for voice chat (such as muting yourself, push to talk key, ...) in the controls from Minecraft itself (at the bottom)
